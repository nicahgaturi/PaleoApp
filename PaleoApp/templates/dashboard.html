Step into the heart of prehistoric discovery with a platform designed for the National Museums of Kenya and the Turkana Basin Institute. This system streamlines accession workflows and prevents double accessioning of fossil specimens — all while saving time and unlocking the secrets of Kenya’s fossil heritage.

KNM WT 15000

The Turkana Boy, also known as KNM-WT 15000, is one of the most complete hominid skeletons ever discovered. It provides invaluable insight into early human evolution and the lifestyle of Homo erectus.
