Welcome to the Paleontology Catalog: Preserving Kenya's Rich Fossil Heritage

Step into the heart of prehistoric discovery with a platform designed for the National Museums of Kenya and the Turkana Basin Institute. This system revolutionizes the accessioning process by streamlining workflows, enhancing speed, accuracy, and efficiency, while preventing double accessioning of fossil specimens. Researchers can catalog with ease, saving time and focusing on what matters most—the science, unlocking the ancient secrets of Kenya's fossil heritage.

KNM WT 15000

The Turkana Boy, also known as KNM-WT 15000, is one of the most complete hominid skeletons ever discovered. It provides invaluable insight into early human evolution and the lifestyle of Homo erectus.
